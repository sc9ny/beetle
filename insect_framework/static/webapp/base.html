Why Insects for Pet?

Dogs and cats definitely are great pets. But sometimes it requires too much of responsibility to take care of them. Then what about reptiles or Tarantula? They are great too, but you sometimes have to feed them live insects/animals. If these are blockers for you to keep pets or if you are simply looking for a exotic pets, maybe consider insects. You can feed them with anything (unless they are carnivours), easy to keep and breed and does not require much responsibility as other pets.
